Startup Name: MediScan AI

Industry: Healthcare AI

Problem:
Hospitals spend significant time manually reviewing radiology reports, causing delays and diagnostic bottlenecks.

Solution:
MediScan AI uses large language models and computer vision to automatically summarize radiology scans and generate preliminary reports.

Business Model:
Subscription-based SaaS platform for hospitals and diagnostic centers.

Target Market:
Healthcare providers in North America and Europe.

Funding Stage:
Seed

Founders:
Dr. Sarah Johnson, former radiologist.
Alex Chen, ex-Google ML Engineer.
